# About the Author

<div class="author-container">

<div class="author-bio">


**Lisa English** is a survivor, a storyteller, and the architect of her own freedom. From the stages of the 1980s escort world to the boardrooms of modern business, she has navigated the economics of power with grit and grace. "Paid In Full" is her unvarnished testimony—a story about breaking the glass, grabbing the knife, and rewriting the end of the script. She lives in [City, State] with the peace she fought forty years to earn.
