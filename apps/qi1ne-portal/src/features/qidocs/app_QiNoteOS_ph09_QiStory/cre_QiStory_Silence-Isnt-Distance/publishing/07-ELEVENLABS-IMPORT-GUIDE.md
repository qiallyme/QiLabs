## 📤 HOW TO IMPORT INTO ELEVENLABS READER

### Step 1: Access ElevenLabs Reader
1. Go to [ElevenLabs Reader](https://elevenlabs.io/app/reader)
2. Sign in to your account
3. Click "Upload Document" or "Import"

### Step 2: Upload Your File
1. Select `Silence-Isnt-Distance-PLAINTEXT.txt` or `Silence-Isnt-Distance-READING-MODE.txt`
2. Wait for upload to complete
3. ElevenLabs will process the text (usually takes 1-2 minutes)

### Step 3: Configure Voice Settings
1. Choose your preferred voice
2. Adjust speed/pacing if needed
3. Set any pronunciation preferences

### Step 4: Start Reading
1. Click "Start Reading" or "Play"
2. The AI will read your book with natural pacing
3. You can pause, skip, or adjust settings at any time

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

### If upload fails:
- Check file size (should be under 10MB)
- Verify file is .txt format
- Ensure UTF-8 encoding

### If reading quality is poor:
- Try the other file version
- Adjust voice settings in ElevenLabs
- Check for any remaining special characters

### If chapters aren't recognized:
- Use PLAINTEXT.txt version (has explicit chapter markers)
- ElevenLabs may not auto-detect chapters in continuous version
