### Step 3: Upload Files

**Manuscript:**
- Upload: `export/Silence-Isnt-Distance-ELEVENREADER.txt`
- Format: Plain Text (.txt)
- File size: ~80KB (well under limits)

**Cover Image:**
- Upload your cover image
- Recommended: 3000x3000px minimum
- Format: PNG or JPG

### Step 4: Add Metadata

**Short Summary (max 500 characters):**
```
A guide to the misunderstood inner world of deep feelers—people who vanish when overwhelmed, shutdown when overloaded, and love with frightening intensity. This book explains why silence is not distance but a nervous system seeking safety. A roadmap for anyone who thinks too much, feels too deeply, or disappears to survive.
```

**Long Summary:**
Use the back-of-book summary from your manuscript, or:

```
People who feel deeply often disappear. Not because they don't care, not because they're rejecting anyone — but because their internal world floods faster than most people can imagine. Silence Isn't Distance is a compassionate, psychology-informed guide to understanding the quiet ones: the overthinkers, the overwhelmed, the hyper-empathic souls who shut down to cope.

Drawing from trauma research, neuroscience, attachment theory, and lived experience, Cody Rice-Velasquez reveals why some people retreat when emotions rise — and how to build relationships that honor both connection and capacity.

You'll learn why "too sensitive" is a myth and bandwidth is the real culprit, how freeze, fawn, and shutdown actually work inside the nervous system, what triggers overwhelm (and how to plan around them), how to love someone whose silence is protective, not punishing, and how to heal survival patterns and reclaim your inner world.

This book is a map — not just for Deep Feelers, but for anyone who loves them. Silence isn't distance. It's depth. It's protection. And it can become home again.
```

### Step 5: Configure Monetization

- **Toggle ON** if you want to earn per listen
- **Toggle OFF** if you want it free

### Step 6: Preview

- ElevenReader will show you:
  - Chapter separation
  - AI narration preview
  - Formatting check

**Review carefully:**
- Check that chapters are properly separated
- Listen to a sample narration
- Verify disclaimer appears correctly

### Step 7: Publish

- Click **"Publish"** or **"Submit for Review"**
- Your book becomes searchable within 24 hours
- You'll receive a confirmation email

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

### If upload fails:
- Check file size (should be under 10MB)
- Verify UTF-8 encoding
- Ensure no special characters causing issues

### If chapters aren't detected:
- Verify `<reader-chapter-break>` markers are present
- Check that chapter titles use `#` format

### If narration sounds off:
- Check that "One Truth" was replaced with "Lesson"
- Verify spacing between paragraphs
- Review chapter break markers
