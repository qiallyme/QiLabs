# Media Prompts for "Paid In Full" Interactive Book

## Key Scenes & Chapter Covers

### 1. The Mirror (Prologue & 06_Epilogue)
**Image Prompt:** A high-end, cinematographic close-up of a shattered mirror in a dark, atmospheric room. The camera focus is on the jagged edges of a cracked glass surface. Resting on the vanity in front of the mirror is a single, worn Motorola pager and a pair of six-inch clear acrylic high heels. Lighting is dramatic "noir" style with deep shadows and a hint of red neon light leaking in from an unseen window. 8k resolution, photorealistic, moody.
**Video Prompt:** A slow-motion panning shot starting from the worn, calloused feet of a woman in clear high heels, moving up to her face reflected in a cracked dressing room mirror. She is forty-six, with a faint but distinct scar running from her lip to her cheek. She looks at her reflection with a steady, unbreakable gaze. The lighting shifts from warm, nostalgic amber to a cold, modern blue.

### 2. The Coal Dust Porch (Chapter 1)
**Image Prompt:** A wide, atmospheric shot of a small, weather-beaten house in rural Southern Indiana, late 1960s. The sky is an oppressive, metallic grey. A seven-year-old girl with blonde hair sits on the dirt in the backyard, her hands coal-stained black. She is focusing intensely on building a tiny fortress of twigs. In the background, the looming, windowless silhouette of a monolithic factory (RCA plant) dominates the horizon, chimneys belching grey smoke.
**Video Prompt:** A low-angle, slow-motion shot of a black coal train thundering past a small backyard. The camera stays on the face of the young girl as the vibration rattles her teeth. Coal dust falls like black snow over her twig fortress. 

### 3. The Black Guitar & The Monster (Chapter 6 & 12)
**Image Prompt:** A 1980s dive bar interior, thick with cigarette smoke and red-gel stage lights. A handsome, dark-haired man with premature grey hair stands center stage, eyes closed, singing passionately into a vintage microphone. He is holding a sleek, solid black electric guitar. The atmosphere is electric and dangerous. In the front row, a young woman (18) watches him with a mixture of adoration and dawning fear.
**Video Prompt:** A quick-cut montage: The man singing on stage / The black guitar crashing onto a wooden floor / A white poodle puppy running on a concrete patio / A heavy hand slamming into a wooden dresser corner. The sound is the rhythmic thumping of a heartbeat.

### 4. The Bus Ride (Chapter 13)
**Image Prompt:** Interior of a Greyhound bus at night, moving through the Southwestern desert. The young woman (20) leans her forehead against the cold window pane. The glass reflects her bruised, healing face and the distant, lonely orange lights of a desert town. The cabin is dark, lit only by the faint green glow of the dashboard.
**Video Prompt:** A point-of-view shot from the bus window as the sharp, orange angles of the desert mountains dissolve into the flat, grey, humid horizons of the Midwest. The sound is the constant, low-frequency hum of a diesel engine.

### 5. The Denny's Meeting (Chapter 14)
**Image Prompt:** A cinematic wide shot of a Denny's parking lot at 3:00 AM under harsh yellow sodium lights. A sleek, cream-colored silk-shirted man (Jaye) leans against a battered sedan. A young woman sits inside, looking out through an inch-open window. The air is thick with humidity and freeway haze. 
**Video Prompt:** Jaye tapping on the car window in slow motion. The young woman's hand dropping to the floorboard to grip a rusted tire iron. The contrast between his polished shoes and her dirt-stained upholstery.

### 6. The Pager Economy (Chapter 15 & 23)
**Image Prompt:** A top-down, "flat lay" arrangement on a glass coffee table in a low-lit 80s apartment. One Motorola pager displaying the code "911", a blue-ink ledger book with handwritten names and room numbers, a glass pipe, and a stack of twenty-dollar bills. A hand with perfectly manicured long red nails is reaching for the pager.
**Video Prompt:** The pager vibrating on a bedside table in the dark, the green screen illuminating the room in pulses. A female hand picks it up. Cut to: A hotel room hallway, a door closing, the click of a deadbolt.

### 7. The Final Stand (Chapter 29)
**Image Prompt:** The interior of a dark taxi cab at night. A terrifying, high-tension close-up of a silver serrated knife glinting in the pale blue light of a cell phone screen. A woman's hand is gripping the blade itself, blood dripping down her wrist. The background shows the blurred, panicked lights of a passing car.
**Video Prompt:** A visceral, shaky-cam sequence in the cab: The locking of doors (click) / The lunge / The woman's teeth sinking into a black leather glove / The cab swerving toward a group of trailer lights. 

### 8. The Madam's Reflection (Epilogue)
**Image Prompt:** A portrait of a 56-year-old woman sitting in a modern, well-lit office. She wears an elegant dark suit. The scar on her face is visible but not disfiguring—it looks like a part of her architecture. She is writing in a leather-bound journal. Behind her, a large window shows a peaceful, daylight view of the Indiana horizon.
**Video Prompt:** The woman walking away from a silver dancing pole in an empty club. She doesn't look back. She walks out into the sunlight. The screen fades to white with the title: **PAID IN FULL: FROM POLE TO POWER**.
