## PRESS HOOK

This book reframes "silence" from rejection to regulation — redefining how we interpret emotional retreat in relationships. It bridges the gap between neuroscience, trauma theory, and lived experience to help Deep Feelers and their loved ones understand what's really happening during shutdown.
