# PAID IN FULL: Distribution Strategy
**Status:** LOCKED
**Approach:** Indie + Direct (Zero-Cash, 100% Constraints)

---

## 1. THE GOAL
*   **Cost:** $0
*   **Rights:** 100% Retained
*   **Control:** Absolute (Price, Covers, Updates)
*   **Model:** Direct-to-Reader + Wide Distribution
*   **No Traditional Gatekeepers**

---

## 2. THE STACK

### A. Master Source
*   **Input:** `FINAL_SUBMISSION/Paid_In_Full_Master_Manuscript.md`
*   **Outputs:**
    *   EPUB (E-book)
    *   PDF (Print/Digital)
    *   DOCX (Formatting intermediary)

### B. Primary Distributor (Wide)
**Platform:** **Draft2Digital (D2D)**
*   **Cost:** Free (retains small % of sales)
*   **Channels:** Apple Books, Barnes & Noble, Kobo, Google Play, Libraries (OverDrive, Hoopla).
*   **ISBN:** Use free D2D ISBN.
*   **Constraint:** **Do NOT enable Amazon distribution here.**

### C. Amazon Strategy (Direct)
**Platform:** **Amazon KDP**
*   **Reason:** Direct control, faster updates, better analytics.
*   **Formats:**
    *   E-book (EPUB upload)
    *   Paperback (PDF upload)
*   **Constraint:** **NO KDP Select** (Keep non-exclusive to allow D2D).

### D. Direct Channel (The Powerhouse)
**Platform:** **Cloudflare Pages / GitHub Pages** (Current Infrastructure)
*   **Assets:**
    *   Landing Page
    *   Interactive Booklet (`/interactive/reader.html`)
    *   Sample Chapters
*   **Monetization:** Direct links to Stripe/Gumroad for PDF/EPUB sales (higher margin).
*   **Audio:** Host excerpts directly.
