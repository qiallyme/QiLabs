## LONG DESCRIPTION (Amazon Listing)

People who feel deeply often disappear.

Not because they don't care, not because they're rejecting anyone — but because their internal world floods faster than most people can imagine. *Silence Isn't Distance* is a compassionate, psychology-informed guide to understanding the quiet ones: the overthinkers, the overwhelmed, the hyper-empathic souls who shut down to cope.

Drawing from trauma research, neuroscience, attachment theory, and lived experience, Cody Rice-Velasquez reveals why some people retreat when emotions rise — and how to build relationships that honor both connection and capacity.

You'll learn:

* Why "too sensitive" is a myth and bandwidth is the real culprit
* How freeze, fawn, and shutdown actually work inside the nervous system
* What triggers overwhelm (and how to plan around them)
* How to love someone whose silence is protective, not punishing
* How to heal survival patterns and reclaim your inner world

This book is a map — not just for Deep Feelers, but for anyone who loves them.

Silence isn't distance. It's depth. It's protection.

And it can become home again.
