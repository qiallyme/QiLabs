Business Return Checklist

Declaración de Negocio

For LLCs, S-Corps, and Partnerships requiring a separate return (1065, 1120-S).
Para entidades como LLC, S-Corp o Sociedades.

Company Info Información

  • Entity Documents / Documentos Legales Articles of Incorporation, Operating Agreement, EIN Letter (if new).
  • Prior Year Business Return / Declaración Anterior Mandatory for tracking basis, assets, and carryovers.
  • Ownership Changes Details if any partners/shareholders entered or left in 2024.

Financials Finanzas

  • Profit & Loss (P&L) Income and Expenses statement for 2024.
  • Balance Sheet / Hoja de Balance Ending balances for bank accounts, loans, and assets as of Dec 31, 2024.
  • General Ledger (If available) Detailed transaction list.

Payroll & 1099s Nómina

  • Payroll Forms (941/940/W-3) Copies of all quarterly and annual payroll filings.
  • 1099s Issued or Received Copies of 1099-NEC forms you issued to contractors.

Common Questions / Preguntas Frecuentes

What is BOI Reporting? / ¿Qué es el reporte BOI?
It's a new mandatory report for 2024/2025 for all LLCs and Inc. We can file this for you as part of your return.
Es un nuevo reporte obligatorio para 2024/2025 para todas las LLCs e Inc. Podemos presentarlo como parte de su declaración.
When are S-Corp returns due? / ¿Cuándo vencen las S-Corp?
March 15th (one month before personal returns).
El 15 de marzo (un mes antes de las declaraciones personales).
