ITIN Services Checklist

Nuevo ITIN o Renovación

Applying for a new ITIN or renewing an existing one.
Solicitud de nuevo ITIN o renovación de uno existente.

Important: Original documents or certified copies are required by the IRS.
Importante: El IRS requiere documentos originales o copias certificadas.

Identification / Identificación Requerido

  • Unexpired Passport / Pasaporte Vigente The only standalone document accepted. Must be original.
  • Or: Birth Certificate + 2nd ID If no passport: Civil birth certificate plus a photo ID (voter card, driver's license).

Dependents (If applying for them) Dependientes

  • Original Birth Certificates / Actas de Nacimiento Required for all children applying.
  • Proof of US Presence / Pruebas de Presencia For dependents living in the US: School records (Report Cards) or Medical records showing US address.

Other Items Otros

  • Renewal Notice (CP48) If you received a letter saying your ITIN is expiring.
  • Income Documents W-2s, 1099s, etc. (ITIN applications must be filed WITH a tax return).

Common Questions / Preguntas Frecuentes

Do I need to send my original passport? / ¿Necesito enviar mi pasaporte original?
Yes, the IRS requires original documents or certified copies from the issuing agency. We can help you certify them.
Sí, el IRS requiere documentos originales o copias certificadas por la agencia emisora. Podemos ayudarle a certificarlos.
How long does it take? / ¿Cuánto tiempo tarda?
ITIN processing can take 7 to 11 weeks depending on IRS volume.
El procesamiento del ITIN puede tardar de 7 a 11 semanas dependiendo del volumen del IRS.
