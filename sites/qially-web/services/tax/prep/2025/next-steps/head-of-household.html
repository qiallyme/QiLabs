Head of Household Checklist

Lista: Cabeza de Familia

For single filers paying more than 50% of household costs for a dependent.
Para solteros/as que pagan más del 50% de los gastos del hogar para un dependiente.

Required Documents Documentos Requeridos

  • Proof of Household Costs Rent/Lease agreement, utility bills, or mortgage statement in YOUR name.
  • Dependent Info Social Security cards and birth dates for all children/dependents.
  • Proof of Residency (School/Medical) School record or medical statement showing child's address matches yours.
  • All Income Forms (W-2, 1099) From all jobs worked in 2024.

If Applicable Si Aplica

  • School tuition (1098-T) If dependent is in college.
  • Daycare Expenses Total paid + Provider's Name, Address, and EIN/SSN.
  • Form 8332 Release of claim to exemption (if non-custodial parent is claiming child).

Common Questions / Preguntas Frecuentes

Who qualifies for Head of Household? / ¿Quién califica?
You must be unmarried (or considered unmarried), pay more than 50% of household costs, and have a qualifying dependent live with you for more than half the year.
Debe ser soltero/a, pagar más del 50% de los gastos del hogar y tener un dependiente calificado viviendo con usted más de medio año.
Can I claim my boyfriend/girlfriend? / ¿Puedo reclamar a mi pareja?
Usually NO for Head of Household status. They may qualify as a dependent in some cases, but they do rarely qualify you for HoH status unless they are related by blood or marriage.
