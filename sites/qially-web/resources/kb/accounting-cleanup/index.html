Mastering the Cleanup: How We Fix Messy Books

Clean financial records are the foundation of any successful business. Our cleanup process is designed to bring total accuracy to your ledger.

Identifying the "Bloat"

Messy books are usually a result of too many accounts, duplicate vendors, and un-reconciled transactions. Our first step is to prune the chart of accounts to reflect a clean flow of money.

Reconciliation is King

We verify every single penny against bank statements and credit card records. If the statement doesn't match the software, we find out why.

The Result: Audit-Ready Financials

At the end of a project, you receive a clean Profit & Loss statement and Balance Sheet. These aren't just numbers—they are verified, defendable financial records.
