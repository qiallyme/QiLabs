III. Legal Protections

Under federal law (ADA Titles II & III; FHA 42 U.S.C. 3601):

  1. Exemption from Fees: Service animals are exempt from all pet fees, pet rent, and security deposits.
  2. Exemption from Restrictions: Service animals are exempt from "no-pet" policies and breed/weight restrictions.
  3. Public Access: Businesses may only ask: (1) Is the dog a service animal required because of a disability? and (2) What work or task has the dog been trained to perform?

Note: Documentation or "certification" is NOT required for public access under the ADA. This assertion is provided to facilitate smooth accommodation in housing and business environments.
