What it is

QiAlly Flows is a next-generation workflow orchestration platform designed for speed, reliability, and ease of use. It allows you to connect your tools, automate repetitive tasks, and visualize complex logic without the overhead of traditional systems.

What it does

  • Event-Driven Triggers: React to data changes in real-time.
  • Multi-Step Logic: Handle complex branching and conditional paths.
  • Secure Integration: Connect to your favorite APIs with encrypted credentials.
  • Performance Monitoring: Keep track of every execution with detailed logs.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: Insert approval steps and manual checkpoints easily.

Security Note: The Flows Engine itself is a private, authenticated workspace. This information site is public-facing and provides general product and legal information.
