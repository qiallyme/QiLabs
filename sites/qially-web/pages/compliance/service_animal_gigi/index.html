Ongoing Reinforcement & Hardening

Gigi has completed all primary modules with Zaitullah Khebarkhil. The following hardening drills are practiced weekly:

  • Public Access Proofing: High-distraction exposure (malls/food courts) to ensure "invisible dog" focus.
  • Extended DPT: Increasing pressure duration (10+ mins) to simulate full episode management.
  • Emergency Off-Leash Recall: Immediate return drill in enclosed safe environments.
  • Handler-Down Simulation: Alerting and staying close during simulated incapacitation.

Core Specialized Tasks

  • Medical Alert (Nudge): Snaps handler out of pre-panic states (bouncing leg/face rubbing).
  • Deep Pressure Therapy (DPT): Lowers heart rate and regulates breathing during active episodes.
  • Blocking: Creates physical space in crowded environments to prevent sensory overload.
  • Grounding: Physical stimulation (Visit/Touch) to break dissociative states.
