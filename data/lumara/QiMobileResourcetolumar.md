## **2. Company Description**

**Industry Background:**
Mobile and gig-based services are projected to grow 15% annually through 2030, driven by the rise in flexible work, DIY homeowners, and on-demand convenience culture. Platforms like TaskRabbit, Angi, and Uber dominate verticals but remain siloed. QiMobileResource™ unifies them under one dispatch ecosystem.

**Company Overview:**
QiMobileResource™ is a technology-first service hub that:

* Operates a digital dispatch platform.
* Markets, routes, and manages jobs for independent mobile professionals.
* Centralizes communication, payment, and CRM tools in one place.
* Offers a low-commission, high-support alternative to large gig platforms.

**Legal Structure:**
LLC registered in Indiana.
Founder holds 100% ownership at launch, with expansion plans for equity-based regional partnerships.
