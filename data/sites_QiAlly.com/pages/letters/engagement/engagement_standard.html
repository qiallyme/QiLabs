1. Parties

This Engagement Letter (“Agreement”) confirms the terms under which QiAlly (“we,” “us,” “our”) will provide professional services to the Client (“you,” “your”).

This Engagement Letter incorporates by reference the QiAlly Master Service Agreement (MSA) and Terms of Service available at https://qially.com/pages/msa/, and the Privacy Policy available at https://qially.com/pages/privacy/. By signing this Engagement Letter electronically, Client acknowledges that they have read, understand, and agree to the terms contained in those policies and that those documents are part of the overall agreement between the parties.

2. Scope of Services

QiAlly will provide professional services as agreed between the parties, which may include accounting, bookkeeping support, tax preparation support, compliance support, advisory, documentation, or administrative services. Services are limited strictly to those expressly agreed.

3. Client Responsibilities

Client is responsible for providing complete and accurate information, timely delivery of requested records, and reviewing all deliverables for accuracy before any filing or submission. QiAlly relies on Client-provided information and does not audit or verify it unless expressly agreed in writing.

4. Fees and Payment

Fees are based on the scope, complexity, and time required. Payment terms will be communicated separately or at the time of service. Services may be paused for non-payment. Fees are earned upon performance, not upon outcome.

5. Deadlines

QiAlly will make reasonable efforts to meet deadlines provided Client supplies required information on time. Delays caused by incomplete or late information are not the responsibility of QiAlly.

6. Confidentiality and Electronic Communication

QiAlly maintains reasonable confidentiality practices. Client acknowledges electronic communication risks and consents to reasonable electronic methods (email, portals, e-signature).

Client understands and agrees that the handling, storage, and processing of personal information will be governed by the QiAlly Privacy Policy, as amended from time to time, available at https://qially.com/pages/privacy/, and that QiAlly may use third-party services in compliance with that policy.

7. No Guarantee of Outcome

No outcomes are guaranteed. Acceptance or results depend on agencies, facts, and Client cooperation.

8. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, QiAlly’s liability is limited to the fees paid for the specific services giving rise to the claim. QiAlly is not liable for indirect or consequential damages.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate with written notice. Fees for services performed remain due.

10. Agreement References & Entire Agreement

This Agreement represents the entire understanding between the parties and may be amended only in writing. Electronic signatures are legally binding.
